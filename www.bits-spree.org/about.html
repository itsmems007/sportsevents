  • About AAVEG:

    AAVEG'19- The Combat Zone is a National level sports fest organized by Invertis University on 14,15,16,17 october 2019. We aim at inculcating the spirit of sportmanship and propagating the spirit of team work harmony and peace. over a span of 3 high intensity days,the festival will hold a large number of events.The participants of every sport have a great opportunity to showcase their talents and grab exciting cash prizes. Aaveg,the sports fest of Invertis University is organized with the sole aim of promoting sports and social interactions amongst colleges from India.As the dust clears over last Aaveg,a fresh challenge looms.The new sporting season has now kicked off, and the ultimate arena to test the challengers is coming to Invertis University,this October 2019 It will be a sight to see who the titan in the world of sports becomes, and who will deserve a place on the mantle of winners this Aaveg'19:The Combat Zone.."


    About Invertis University:

    Invertis is a leading university offering wide range of courses pertaining to different fields of study. From a humble beginning in 1998, with one institute namely Invertis Institute of management studies and 83 students, it has emerged as fully fledged with nine institutes and 6000 students under its name. Invertis offers doctoral, post graduate and under graduate programmes in Education, Management, Computer Application, Engineering, Architecture, Applied science and Humanities, Law, Pharmacy, Journalism &Mass Communication and Fashion Design along with Diploma programmes in Engineering. Invertis believe that success of an institution comes along with the achievement of its students. Our faculty member fosters knowledge and skills among students to prepare them to meet the challenges of professional world. 'Holistic Development' is the key for our learning modules as we focus on an all-round development of our students through sports activity, workshops, fashion shows and cultural programs etc.We believe in learning beyond classroom.
